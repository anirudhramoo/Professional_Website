Check out Optifocal drive

Optifocal drive is a computer vision application I co-kickstarted, aimed at accurately identifying a driver and then proctoring their attention to ensure that they stay alert and focused.

Built primarily with OpenCV, Python, SciPy and the Dlib facial landmarking model, Optifocal drive can accurately estimate the pose of a driver's head to determine their level of attention.

Here are some of my projects

Eye-Alarm

Developed with React, Eye-Alarm makes use of the TensorFlow MediaPipe face-mesh model to prevent users from accidentally falling asleep. This application locates a user’s facial features (most notably their eyes), and calculates their Eye Aspect Ratio (EAR) to determine if their eyes have been closed for an extended period of time. If so, loud music would soon be played.

WinAway

WinAway is a full stack MERN application serving as the only service where YouTubers can advertise their giveaways. Here, YouTubers can sign in to their respective YouTube channels via google OAUTH2 and create social media-like posts for their giveaways, advertising them to a wider audience than their own.

MovieDict

Developed mainly using React, Node Js, and Express, MovieDict uses theMovieDB API to serve as the ultimate media dictionary. It allows users to find movies and shows similar to their top picks, as well as those containing their favorite actors/actresses. Moreover, it also provides the platforms on which said entertainment is available on, depending on a user's location.
